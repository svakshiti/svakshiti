About Swakshiti

Based in Vadodara, India, Swakshiti is a pioneering venture that provides innovative vertical farming solutions for smaller spaces such as households. Founded by a group of six friends, our mission is to bring the farm-to-fork concept into homes. We also provide comprehensive educational resources on vertical farming to empower individuals to grow their own produce.
